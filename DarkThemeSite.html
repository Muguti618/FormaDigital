Our Story

Evergreen Bistro was born from a passion for sustainable dining and community connection.

Inspired by Nature, Driven by Community

Founded in 2015 by Chef Maria Rodriguez, Evergreen Bistro began as a small neighborhood cafe with a big vision: to create a dining experience that celebrates local producers and sustainable practices.

Today, we're proud to partner with over 20 local farms and producers to bring you a menu that changes with the seasons and reflects the bounty of our region.

Our kitchen is led by award-winning Chef Elena Martinez, who believes that exceptional food begins with exceptional ingredients and a deep respect for the environment.
