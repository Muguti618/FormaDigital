Our Story

Antic Pitarra was born from a passion for authentic Spanish cuisine and family tradition.

Since 1920 in Barcelona's Gothic Quarter

Founded in 1920 by the Pitarra family, our restaurant began as a small tavern serving traditional Catalan dishes to locals in the Gothic Quarter.

Today, we're proud to continue the family legacy, serving authentic Spanish cuisine with recipes passed down through four generations.

Our kitchen is led by Chef Javier Martínez, who combines traditional techniques with the finest local ingredients from Catalan markets.
