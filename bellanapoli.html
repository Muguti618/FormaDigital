Our Story

Founded in 1985 by the Rossi family, Bella Napoli brings the authentic flavors of Naples to your table. What started as a small family trattoria in the heart of Italy has grown into a beloved restaurant known for its traditional recipes and warm hospitality.

We believe in using only the freshest ingredients, many of which are imported directly from Italy. Our pasta is made fresh daily, and our pizza is cooked in a traditional wood-fired oven, just like in Naples.

Our chefs, trained in the art of Italian cuisine, bring passion to every dish they create. From our classic Margherita pizza to our homemade tiramisu, each item on our menu tells a story of tradition and love for food.
